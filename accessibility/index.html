Our commitment

Grayson Kendall (doing business as Closora) is committed to making Closora usable by everyone, including people with disabilities, and to continually improving accessibility.

Conformance target

We are working toward conformance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, and strive to meet the accessibility expectations of the ADA and equivalent laws.

Status: this is a statement of intent and ongoing effort. We have not yet completed a full third-party accessibility audit, and the app may not yet fully conform in all areas.

Known limitations

We have not yet completed a formal third-party accessibility audit, so specific conformance gaps have not been catalogued. Areas we are actively reviewing include color-contrast ratios, screen-reader labels on custom controls, and dynamic type / font-scaling support.

This list will be updated with specific findings once an audit is completed.

Reporting an issue

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, contact us with the screen and assistive technology you were using: graysonkendall3@gmail.com.

We aim to acknowledge reports within 5 business days and provide a substantive response within 30 days.

Feedback & alternative formats

If you need information in an alternative format or have suggestions, contact graysonkendall3@gmail.com. Your feedback helps us prioritize improvements.
