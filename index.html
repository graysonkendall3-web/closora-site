What is Closora?

A daily habit for closers.

Closora turns sales practice into a game you actually want to open. Build a streak with quick daily lessons, earn XP, and climb the weekly leaderboard — the same loop that makes language apps addictive, pointed at the skills that win deals.

Then put it to work: jump into an AI roleplay call with a simulated prospect, handle the objections, and get a coaching report the moment you hang up. Reps managing a team get Manager Mode to keep everyone sharp. No quotas to hit here — just skills to sharpen.

How it works

Learn it. Practice it. Own it.

Bite-size quizzes and flashcards drill the fundamentals — discovery, framing, objection handling, closing. Every session feeds your streak and your rank.

Roleplay

AI practice calls with prospects who push back

Dial into a simulated prospect and run the whole conversation out loud. The AI stays in character — stalls, objects, and tests your framing across five industries.

Every call ends with a score, the moments you nailed, and exactly what to sharpen next time — so each rep is a little tighter than the last.

  • Clear, actionable things to sharpen
